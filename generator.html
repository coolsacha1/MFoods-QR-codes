Setup Instructions

1Deploy gate.html to Vercel (drag & drop the file, or push to a repo).

2Copy your Vercel URL (e.g. mata-gate.vercel.app) and paste it in the field above.

3Generate a QR for each service. Print it and place it at the host stand.

4Customer scans → gate page checks São Paulo time → redirects to waitlist or shows expired.

All time validation uses Brasília time (UTC-3), regardless of the customer's phone timezone.
